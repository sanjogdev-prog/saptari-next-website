Advisory Council

Saptari Next is guided by an Advisory Council — accomplished individuals from Saptari and its diaspora who lend the campaign their judgement, networks and expertise. Advisors serve in a voluntary, honorary capacity; the campaign remains accountable to the district through its Poll pillar.

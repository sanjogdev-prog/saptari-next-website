VILLAGE MODULE 01

Boria बोरिया

The convener's own village and the template for One Village at a Time. The former Boria VDC — once nine wards — now lives in two local governments: Rajbiraj Municipality Ward 16 and Bishnupur Rural Municipality Ward 5. Understanding this one village deeply — its land, water, people, school, shrines and songs — is how the campaign learns to understand every village.

The Five-Year Framework (VDM Ward 16)

The module follows the Comprehensive Village Development Model prepared for Ward 16 — governance, economic and social pillars, monitored through community scorecards and aligned to the SDGs.

Year 1

Baseline surveys and GIS mapping; governance training; urgent health post upgrades and school repairs; pilot vocational training and microfinance.

Year 2

Roads and drainage works; scale health services and school development; agriculture modernisation and irrigation; waste management and solar lighting.

Year 3

Social inclusion and GESI campaigns; entrepreneurship hub and incubation; organic farming; community tourism assets and cultural events.

Year 4

Transition services to local institutions; strengthen monitoring and grievance systems; climate resilience assessment.

Year 5

Handover to community and ward bodies; document learning; After-Action Review and the next five-year plan; local development week exposition.

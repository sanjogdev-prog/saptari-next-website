Alumni Registration

"Once a student, always a member of Narendra Memorial."

Narendra Memorial Janta Secondary School, Boria, is building a lifelong network of every former student, one that offers mentorship, scholarships, career guidance and leadership to the students coming up behind them. Registering takes two minutes and adds you to the school's digital Alumni Registry, the foundation for everything from the Annual Alumni Meet to the Alumni Endowment Fund described in the full Alumni Network & Association plan.

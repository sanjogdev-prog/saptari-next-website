Partner Organisations

Saptari Next works through partnership, not ownership. These organisations work with the campaign — and beyond it — for the betterment of Saptari.

Thako Devi Jogendra Bhagat Sewa Trust (TJB Sewa Trust)

A family philanthropic trust serving Saptari. Its landmark contribution is the donation and construction of the Nepal Police Regional Hospital in Rupani — inaugurated in 2022 — bringing regional health infrastructure to the district. The Trust has also supported the APF School and other community institutions.

The convener's role: Sanjog Dev served as the Trust's acting president in 2022, leading it through the hospital's inauguration, and remains engaged in its overall planning and development — one of the previous works Saptari Next builds upon.

Setubandh — सेतुबन्ध

Bridging hearts and minds together · a civic society movement

Setubandh is a civic society movement that connects Nepal's intellectuals and leading minds in shaping the destiny of the nation together. As part of the campaign, convener Sanjog Dev travelled to about 57 districts of Nepal alongside his mentor Anand Aditya, building the nationwide network of relationships and civic method that Saptari Next now applies at district scale. Setubandh is a partner organisation of Saptari Next.

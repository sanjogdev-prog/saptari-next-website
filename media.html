#SaptariNext Launching

On 18 February 2023 the campaign convener, Sanjog Dev, announced Saptari Next, a citizens' campaign envisioning the Saptari of 2030, 2040 and 2050. The Saptari Next page carried the launch on 28 February 2023.

Speaking about Saptari in the Nepali Emerging Leaders Program (Leadership Academy Nepal with the Kroc Institute, University of San Diego).

Hospital Inauguration, Rupani

Inauguration of the Nepal Police Regional Hospital in Rupani, donated by Thako Devi Jogendra Bhagat Sewa Trust, where the convener served as acting president (2022) and remains engaged in its planning and development.

Global Perspectives

Hosting the English TV show Global Perspectives (with Dr. Walter CK Wong), aired on national television.

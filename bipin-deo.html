Bipin Deo is from Boria and now teaches English at Amrit Science Campus (ASCOL), a constituent campus of Tribhuvan University in Kathmandu. Alongside teaching, he writes as a geopolitics and diplomacy analyst, publishing opinion pieces on South Asian and great-power politics — India–US relations, India–Japan ties, Pakistan–Afghanistan and Bangladesh's shifting alignments among them — in Nepali and English outlets. He has also appeared on the campaign's own Gaamak Baat series, in conversation about Boria.

This is a living profile. It currently lists only sources that have been independently verified — his public byline confirmed on each outlet, or a link shared directly for this page. If you know of more of his interviews, writings, or a photo, let us know and we'll verify and add it.
